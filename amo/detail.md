ClickTabMove moves tabs to another window or a new window from Firefox's right-click menu.

You can move:

- The clicked tab
- Tabs on the right side of the clicked tab
- The clicked tab and tabs on the right side
- Tabs on the left side of the clicked tab
- The clicked tab and tabs on the left side
- All tabs
- Tabs selected in a popup window

Moving all tabs in one window to another window can merge the two windows.
Pinned tabs remain pinned after moving.

Optional notifications can show progress and completion.
The notification permission is requested only when notifications are enabled in settings.
